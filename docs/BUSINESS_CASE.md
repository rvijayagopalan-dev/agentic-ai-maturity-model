# Business Case & ROI — AI Evolution & Maturity Platform

## 1. Executive Summary

This business case presents the financial and strategic justification for a phased enterprise AI program using the 10-level AI maturity model, applied to Customer Support & Service Operations. The program delivers a projected **3-year NPV of $14.2M** and **payback period of 14 months**, based on modelled cost avoidance, revenue protection, and operational efficiency gains.

---

## 2. Problem Statement

| Pain Point | Current State | Business Impact |
|---|---|---|
| High support cost | 500 FTE agents; $45M/year cost | Cost growth of 8% YoY |
| Long resolution times | Average 24-hour resolution | 18% CSAT below industry benchmark |
| Knowledge silos | Policies in 12 systems; inconsistent answers | 23% repeat contact rate |
| Slow escalation | Manual routing; 40% misdirected | Agent frustration; re-work cost |
| Limited 24/7 coverage | Business hours only in some regions | Lost international revenue |

---

## 3. Proposed Solution

A phased AI program across 9 phases (Level 1 → Level 10) that progressively automates customer support operations while maintaining quality, compliance, and human oversight.

---

### 4.2 Benefits by Phase (Annual)

| Phase Active | Benefit Type | Driver | Annual Value |
|---|---|---|---|
| Phase 1 (L1–2) | Agent productivity | AI-assisted drafting saves 10 min/case | $450K |
| Phase 2 (L3) | Deflection | Self-service containment +15% | $1.35M |
| Phase 3 (L4) | Resolution time | Automated lookups save 8 min/case | $720K |
| Phase 4 (L5) | Process automation | 30% of standard workflows automated | $2.7M |
| Phase 5 (L6) | FTE reduction | -30 FTEs (redeployment, not redundancy) | $2.7M |
| Phase 6 (L7) | First-contact resolution | FCR +25% → lower repeat contacts | $2.25M |
| Phase 7 (L8) | Full process automation | 60% of cases end-to-end AI handled | $5.4M |
| Phase 8 (L9) | AI workforce scale | 24/7 coverage; international expansion | $3.6M |
| Phase 9 (L10) | Self-optimisation | Continuous improvement; reduced CoE cost | $1.8M |

*FTE cost basis: $90K fully loaded average annual cost per support agent*
*Benefits are additive and cumulative across phases*

## 6. Cost Avoidance

|---|---|---|---|
